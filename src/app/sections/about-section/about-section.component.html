What is Circuit Buddy ?

Circuit Buddy is all about Having Fun with Building Real Circuits and making it more interesting to Learn Electronics for beginners.

You can start from simpler and Beginner friendly ones and move towards Advanced and more challenging circuits.

Instructions, Schematics and Important details to learn, are available in circuitbuddy.github.io.

Circuit Buddy is a project from Sri Lanka.

Why Type-C USB ?

  • USB Type-C power adapters are safer to use, a perfect option for experiments with extremely low risk of getting hurt.

  • Easier to use, and no need to meddling with wires, which is messy and unreliable. Just plug it in and watch it come alive.

  • Type-C has become the global standard for power adapters. Wide availability allows you to power up your circuit anywhere

What is Circuit Buddy?

Circuit Buddy is an open project for learning electronics by building real circuits, not just simulating them. Each kit is a small, single-purpose PCB — a blinker, a siren, a touch switch — designed to teach one idea at a time.

Every board is numbered like a collectible. Start with PCB No: 01 and work your way up: the circuits slowly introduce new components, from resistors and capacitors to transistors and timer ICs.

All schematics, guides, and firmware live right here on GitHub — free to read, remix, and share.
